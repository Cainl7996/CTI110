Our Mission

Our goal with this cafe is to provide a welcoming place for those very victims to meet others like them, thus creating new bonds and friendships. The age range would consist mostly of people from the ages of 12 to 25. Many youths come up with clubs of their own at schools that often times are turned down. And with our clubhoouse/cafe, we plan to provide a safe space for these youths to spend time creating friendships with others that they wouldn't have otherwise conversed with on a daily basis. We hope to bred a new generation of more comfortable and sociable fans.

How We Work
